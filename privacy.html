KILL DULL. PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: June 2026

This is a placeholder privacy policy for Kill Dull and the Pitch Against Dull tool. A full policy will be published before public launch.

What we collect

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, company, and role. When you run a Truth Detector session, we store your brief inputs and session results in our database so you can access them later.

We use cookies and browser local storage to keep you signed in between visits and to remember your cookie consent preference.

How we use it

Your information is used solely to operate the Pitch Against Dull tool and to improve the experience. We do not sell your data or share it with third parties for advertising purposes.

Third-party services

We use Firebase (Google) for authentication and database storage. By using this tool you are also subject to Google's Privacy Policy. Session inputs may be sent to the OpenAI API to generate analysis and provocations.

Data retention

Session data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data by emailing human@killdull.com.

Contact

Questions about this policy: human@killdull.com
