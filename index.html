The Brief

You get three random words.
Non-negotiable. Use all of them in your
idea somehow.
The Pitch
90 seconds. Out loud.
In the office, on a video call, or on the phone. No slides. No safety net. Just you and the idea.
The Vote
No self-voting. No politics. Vote for
the idea you'd most want to make.
Most votes wins the round.
The Winner
First to win 3 rounds wins.
Loser buys the drinks.
